How We Collect Phone Numbers for SMS

We collect phone numbers directly through Instagram DMs during appointment booking conversations. Our team member will ask:

“Can I get your mobile number so we can also send you an appointment confirmation via SMS?”

After receiving the number, we follow up with:

“Thanks! Just a heads up that we’ll send your appointment time to this number, in addition to your calendar invite and Instagram messages.”

We also explain that this is optional and easy to stop anytime:

“If you ever want to stop receiving appointment details by SMS, just let us know here on Instagram or via text.”

Message Frequency and Purpose

  • We only send appointment-related messages.
  • Typically, 1–2 SMS messages per appointment are sent.
  • No promotional or marketing texts are sent at this time.

How to Opt Out

Since our process is manual, clients can opt out of SMS messages at any time by:

  • Messaging us on Instagram
  • Texting us directly
  • Informing us during any conversation

We honor all opt-out requests immediately and confirm that you won’t receive further SMS messages.

Your Privacy

Your phone number is only used for appointment notifications and never shared with third parties. For details, see our Privacy Policy.
